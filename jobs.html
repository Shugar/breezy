Now Hiring: Android Software Developer

Background

BreezyPrint Corporation in Oakland, CA, developer of mobile applications for remote printing, is seekingAndroid Software Developers to design and develop our flagship Android applications for secure mobile printing. Individuals will use standard occupational tools, skills and knowledge to perform the following duties:

  • Work closely with the development team to ensure compatibility with existing ApplicationProgramming.
  • Interface (API's) and workflows.
  • Utilize best-of-breed tools and processes to ensure high-quality code.
  • Perform continuous integration and unit testing to assist in providing improved quality assurance (QA).
  • Design and implement new features, create user interfaces (UI), and perform debugging and data modeling.
  • Collaborate across disciplines with team members representing product,design, web development, and product marketing.
  • Maintain our existing Android apps, listen to feedback, and address/resolve issues as they arise.
  • Provide feedback and project status updates to the team.
  • Attend product and user meetings as required.
  • Document program requirements, specifications,test cases, implementation details, and maintenance updates.
  • Integrate our apps with Mobile Application Management (MAM) solutions.

This position does not supervise other employees.

Requirements

  • Education: Bachelor's degree or foreign equivalent in Electronics or Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineers/Science, or a related STEM (science, technology, engineering and/or math) field of study providing the required knowledge and abilities. Will accept three- or four-year degree.
  • Experience: 2 years in a related occupation that provided the required skills and abilities.
  • Skills: Individuals must have demonstrable proficiency, knowledge and experience with the following: Eclipse IDE and Android Studio; Android architecture; hands-on experience in migrating between major versions of Android SDK (e.g., 2.1 to 3.0, 3.0 to 4.0, etc.); software Version Control Systems and Software, with significant prior experience using Git in a professional context; Agile methodologies, such as Scrum; JIRA software; and continuous integration.

Any suitable combination of education, training or experience is acceptable. The term suitable, in this context, means substantial compatibility with stated academic level and job experience requirements and ability to perform job duties.

Job Location:

Oakland, California. Job duties are project-based and performed on long-term assignments at various unanticipated sites within the U.S. which may require relocation at the end of the project. No travel or telecommuting.

To apply:

Mail resume to 160 Franklin St Ste 105, Oakland CA 94607, attn. J. Hansen. No phone calls or e-mails.
