Most Companies Are at Risk for Security Breaches

CIOs know that employees who can’t print from their mobile devices waste valuable time transferring documents or data to desktop or laptop computers – or they find consumer apps that let them print but bypass corporate security and compliance standards.


Analysts say:

  • Nearly two thirds of all companies today are at risk of security breaches due to insecure mobile printing work-arounds.
  • By 2017, 87% of the worldwide smart connected device market will be tablets and smartphone.
  • 32% of employees globally now rely on more than one mobile device during a typical work day.
  • 90% of U.S. organizations experienced leakage or loss of sensitive or confidential documents within the last 12 months.

Hassle-Free Security and
Compliance that Users Love

Breezy is built from the ground up to be easy to use and to provide secure mobile printing that meets or exceeds compliance standards like FERPA, FINRA, HIPAA, and PCI. It’s the only solution that gives employees an easy way to print from any mobile device to any printer without creating compliance or security risks.

Breezy's offers compliance and security benefits, and adds the “last mile” to the data security protection provided by major EMM vendors. It’s also the easiest to use. Best of all from an IT standpoint, it takes less than half an hour to install throughout the company’s IT infrastructure. Users love it because it works the way they expect it to work – and management loves it because it reduces overall company risk and provides valuable cost tracking and control features at the group and individual user level.

End-to-End Security

Breezy security is state of the art, with unique on-device encryption and the industry's widest range of EMM integrations. Documents that leave the mobile device for printing are encrypted even before they travel through our secure channel to the printers inside the organization, ensuring that attackers can't gain access to valuable IP.
