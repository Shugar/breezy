Breezy for Good Brings Secure Mobile Printing to Android

Secure printing for the mobile workforce is now available for Android and iOS devices with Breezy for Good.

 Oakland, CA – January 14, 2013 – Breezy, an industry leader in secure mobile printing, has released an Android version of its “Breezy for Good” app, expanding secure mobile printing flexibility for enterprises utilizing Good Technology™. Whether operating within a deployed or BYOD model Breezy for Good ensures secure mobile printing on iOS and Android devices that harness the Good Dynamics suite of mobile collaboration solutions.providing users the flexibility to use their favorite mobile apps while allowing IT to maintain control over policies.

Breezy for Good on Android is designed to work seamlessly within the Good environment to provide secure mobile printing for the workforce on the go. By integrating the Good Trust™ security platform, Breezy for Good extends critical identity and access management (IAM) capabilities which include strong authentication, identity federation and single sign-on functionality for Good users.

“Breezy for Good eliminates what we refer to as ‘last mile’ vulnerability for Good users, by ensuring that information within the Good environment remains secure during the ‘last mile’ of the journey from digital to printed form”, said Jared Hansen, CEO of Breezy. “We are excited that the rich functionality of Breezy for Good is now available for both Android and iOS users.”

Enterprise users enjoy the power and security of Breezy’s full platform, including its global Partner Network, empowering Good users to print securely from any iOS or Android device to any printer, anywhere, and at any time. CIOs and IT administrators will appreciate:

  • On-device encryption – Breezy is the only mobile print solution that encrypts files on the device before transferring the files via secure channels to the intended printer.
  • Centralized IT Management – Full administrative control and visibility across the enterprise through a web dashboard with control over Breezy for Good app functionality.
  • Simple to Deploy – Breezy can be deployed and running within your Good environment within minutes, with no expensive consultants required.
  • Breezy Partner Network – A global network of thousands of hotels, airports and print service providers enabling users to print securely when on the go.


Built from the ground up with security in mind, Breezy for Good integrates seamlessly with other Good Dynamics-enabled mobile apps, increasing the collaboration and productivity of your mobile workforce.

To get started with the Breezy for Good app, visit: http://www1.good.com/partners/integration/good-dynamicssolutions

Download the Breezy for Good on Android app here: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.breezy.good.an droid

About Breezy

Breezy (www.breezy.com) is the world's leading secure mobile printing platform. Breezy's software lets users to print to any printer from any device, and can mobile-enable existing printer fleets in a matter of minutes.

The Breezy Partner Network, with thousands of locations worldwide, is the world's largest fully secured printing network - and the Breezy API, used in a growing number of popular productivity apps, can add secure printing capabilities to any app on any platform.
