Breezy Joins OpenPeak SECTOR Network

Posted on October 07, 2014

Secure mobile printing leader Breezy today announced that it has joined OpeanPeak?s SECTOR Network. The addition of the leading Android & iOS secure mobile print app allows global service provider EMM solutions to print without sacrificing security.

The SECTOR Network is a secure business app ecosystem endorsed by leading service providers worldwide. The SECTOR Network consists of popular business apps that have undergone a wrapping process to add advanced security features, functionality, and IT management and reporting capabilities. SECTOR Network apps can be easily downloaded by enterprise customers from the most popular public app stores.

Breezy makes companies more mobile-friendly by giving users secure access to any company printer from any mobile device, without added compliance worries. In conjunction with leading enterprise mobility management (EMM) platforms like OpenPeak, Breezy closes the security gap that can be created when users try to print from a mobile device using unsecured print apps designed for consumers.

Jared Hansen, CEO and founder of Breezy, said the company is very excited to combine Breezy?s secure mobile printing solution with OpenPeak to provide enhanced security for customers.

?We?re excited to welcome Breezy, a recognized industry leader in secure mobile printing to the SECTOR Network ecosystem of secure enterprise apps,? said Chick Cairo, Vice President of Alliances at OpenPeak. ?The Breezy for SECTOR app will now be available to business customers of the world?s largest carriers and OEMs that offer the SECTOR Network as part of their solutions for secure enterprise mobility management.?

SECTOR Network Overview

The SECTOR Network is a secure business app ecosystem endorsed by leading service providers worldwide. The network includes popular business apps that have undergone a unique wrapping process to add advanced security features, functionality, and IT management and reporting capabilities.  

SECTOR Network apps are managed, secured and accessed within SECTOR-based enterprise workspaces, including AT&T Toggle, BlackBerry Secure Work Space and Deutsche Telekom SAMBA! SECTOR Network apps can be easily downloaded by enterprise customers from the most popular public app stores.

The Breezy for SECTOR app enables secure mobile printing from iOS and Android devices within the authorized secure mobile workspaces, giving employees the printing capability and ease of use that they want, plus the enterprise data security that IT demands.

Breezy for SECTOR Features

The new Breezy for Sector app wraps Breezy?s proven secure mobile printing functionality with OpenPeak?s consistent authentication, authorization, accounting, and administrative features.

Breezy for SECTOR features include:

  • Data encryption for data at rest, as well as data in motion.
  • Secure communication. All secure apps can interact and share data while preventing access from non-secure apps.
  • Enhanced security. A separate data encryption key per app with app fingerprint validation. All secure apps are fingerprinted to determine the origin and validate the integrity of the app before it can participate in a secure workspace. Secure apps are validated both prior to joining the container and every time they run.
  • Persistent shared app-level VPN. SECTOR Network apps share an active VPN tunnel connection that does not need to be restarted for each app providing a seamless user experience. IT infrastructure only needs to support a single VPN session per device instead of per app.
  • Data lock and wipe. If a device is lost, stolen, or jail broken, or if an employee violates policy, IT admins can remotely lock a workspace, or wipe all enterprise data from a device. 
  • Policy-based app management control. All policy control can be dynamically managed; app policies can be changed remotely at any time by the enterprise.
  • Easily deployed and managed through a centralized administration portal.
